Projects

Some of the projects I've been working. Some of these are projects I've worked on in my spare time, others are projects that are originate at work.

  • Manifold Kubernetes Operator

    A Kubernetes Operator to manage secrets stored in Manifold. This enables your developers to store their credentials securely with Manifold, and load them accordingly in your Kubernetes clusters.
  • Terraform Manifold

    A Terraform provider to manage resources on Manifold, allowing you to store and read credentials through Terraform.
  • Terraform Aiven

    A Terraform provider to provision resources with Aiven. This project has been officially handed off to the Aiven team.
  • Experiment

    A Go package for experimenting with and evaluating new codepaths without impacting your end users.
